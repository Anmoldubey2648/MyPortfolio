BM Fitnessware

BM Fitnessware is a smart fitness platform that helps users take control of their health through personalized features. Based on your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), it generates tailored meal charts and fitness plans aimed at weight loss or muscle gain. The platform also includes features like workout tips, step tracking counters, and options to upload photos or videos for visual tracking. Designed with user convenience in mind, BM Fitnessware combines structure and flexibility to support a healthier lifestyle.
